## Excluded Markets (Why Not?)

### ❌ Spain (Barcelona, etc.)
- Tourist tax complications
- Rental restrictions in major cities
- Regional political uncertainty
- Better alternatives in Portugal/UK

### ❌ Greece (Athens, etc.)
- Recent economic instability
- Complex bureaucracy
- Language barrier
- Lower market transparency

### ❌ Eastern Europe (Budapest, Tallinn, etc.)
- Lower market transparency
- Political risk concerns
- Limited liquidity
- Language barriers

### ❌ Germany (Berlin, etc.)
- Tenant-friendly regulations (challenging for landlords)
- Rent control in major cities
- Complex German legal language
- Lower yields

### ❌ USA (Miami, etc.)
- No residency pathway for investors
- Complex state/federal tax structure
- Foreign investment restrictions (FIRPTA)
- High financing barriers

### ❌ Singapore
- 60% foreign buyer stamp duty (ABSD)
- Severe foreign ownership restrictions
- Prohibitively expensive for most investors
