## Why Invest in UK Cities?

### ✅ Advantages
- **Legal Framework**: Strong property rights, transparent contracts
- **Market Transparency**: Clear data, 8-9/10 transparency scores
- **Language**: English-speaking, no language barrier
- **Liquidity**: Easy to buy/sell (7-8/10 liquidity)
- **Rental Demand**: Universities, young professionals, stable employment
- **Infrastructure**: Excellent transport, healthcare, education

### ⚠️ Considerations
- **No Residency Programs**: Unlike Portugal/Spain Golden Visas
- **Tax**: Standard UK property taxes apply
- **Brexit Impact**: Some uncertainty, but market stabilized
- **Management**: May need property management for overseas investors
